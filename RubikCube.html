Game Instructions:

  1. Select the level for the game (Novice or Medium or Expert).
  2. Select the size of the cube (3X3 or 4X4).
  3. To begin, click on Start.
  4. To reset the cube, click on Start.
  5. For exiting the game, Click on EXIT.
  6. To rotate the camera of cube. click anywhere on the screen (except on cube) and rotate as you like.

CUBE NAVIGATION:

  1. Rotate Right: To move slabs to right, DRAG the entire slab in right direction.
  2. Rotate Left: To move slabs to left, DRAG the entire slab in left direction.
  3. Rotate Up: To move slabs to up, DRAG the entire slab in upward direction.
  4. Rotate Down: To move slabs to down, DRAG the entire slab in downward direction.
